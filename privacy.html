Privacy Policy

BVM Dev Pvt Ltd ("we", "BVM Dev") highly prioritizes your data privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use our software and services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Client name, email address, contact number, and billing details.

Service Data: Business logic, database structures, and content provided by you for software development.

Technical Logs: IP address, browser type, and usage statistics generated automatically while using the software.

2. Data Security & Storage

Encryption: All of your data is protected using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption.

Secure Storage: We only use highly secure cloud servers such as AWS (Amazon Web Services) or Google Cloud.

Access Control: Strict technical controls are followed to ensure only authorized persons can access your data.

3. User Rights

Access & Edit: You may request us at any time to view or make changes to your information.

Right to Erasure: You have the right to completely delete your information from our systems (once the project is completed and dues are settled).

4. Data Sharing & Third-Parties

No Data Sale: We will not sell your information to any third party.

Third-Party Services: Payment Gateways (Stripe/PayHere) and SMS APIs used in the software handle data according to their own privacy policies. BVM Dev Pvt Ltd is not responsible for their practices.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for those under 18 years of age. If any such data is collected unknowingly, we will delete it immediately.

6. Data Breach Notification

If any security breach occurs regarding your data, we will notify you via email within 72 hours.

7. Data Retention

After the contract ends and all dues are settled, your data backups will be handed over to the client or safely deleted according to your request.

8. Changes to Policy

Changes may be made to this privacy policy from time to time. When changes are made, the "Effective Date" will be updated.

9. Contact Us

Please contact us for any doubts regarding this policy.
